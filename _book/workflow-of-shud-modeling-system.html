Solver for Hydrologic Unstructured Domain (SHUD)

Chapter 2 Workflow of SHUD Modeling System

The workflow of modeling with SHUD Modeling System

  1. Prepare raw Essential Terrestrial Variables (ETV)
  2. Convert and crop raw data with the research area boundary.
  3. Build the unstructued modeling domain with SHUD-tools
  4. Run SHUD on desktop or cluster.
  5. Analysis the SHUD model results with SHUD-tools or your hydrologic analysis tools.
