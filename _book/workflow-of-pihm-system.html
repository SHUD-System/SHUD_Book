Chapter 2 Workflow of PIHM System

The workflow of modeling with PIHM System

  1. Prepare raw Essential Terrestrial Variables (ETV)
  2. Convert and crop raw data with the research area boundary.
  3. Build the PIHM modeling domain with PIHMgis or PIHMgisR (Recommended for PIHM++)
  4. Run PIHM on desktop or cluster.
  5. Analysis the PIHM results with PIHMgisR or your hydrologic analysis tools.
